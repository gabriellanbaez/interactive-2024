Live Boricua Embrace where you are.

Experience a joyful, sun-kissed adventure in the Heart and Soul of the Caribbean.

Rich history and culture, exceptional food and cocktails, pristine beaches, majestic mountains, and experiences that nourish body and soul — all packed into one sun-kissed Caribbean paradise. Puerto Rico is full of people who are welcoming and buena gente (which is Boricua lingo for kind and friendly). Here, you’ll feel like family instead of just a visitor. Discover vibrant cultural experiences, a celebration of life, and a captivating rhythm around every corner. Let us be the first to say ¡bienvenidos!

Traveling from the United States?

No Passport Needed!

Since Puerto Rico is a U.S. territory, American citizens and permanent residents can travel to the Island passport free. All you need to bring is a valid ID, such as a driver's license or state ID card.
